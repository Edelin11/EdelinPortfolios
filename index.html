ABOUT ME

I love pink

I came to live in Rhode Island at the age of 15 with my mom and my two brothers. We arrived to the cold night of a December 25th. The first two years went like a blur for me. But everything started to make sence after those years. I graduated high school in 2013 and started college in fall of the same year.
After three years of not know what my passion was and while taking my general education and modern languages classes I stumbled upon computer science.
And the rest is history.


There are these high school in The Dominican Republic in which you have to study a career like a community college but in high school. The options were accounting, marketing, hospitality and tourism, nursing, and computer science.
I wanted to choose computer science because I didn't like the idea of any of the other carrers at all, even though I didn't know anything about CS at that time. I wasn't able to study it because it starts in the eleventh grade and I left in the middle of the tenth grade. Fast forward to three semesters before my college graduation and that is when I realised that my life went in full circle and I ended up studying what I wanted to in high school all those years ago.
Life is funny.
